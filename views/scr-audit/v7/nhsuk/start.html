Use this service to check where your Summary Care Record has been used.

Your Summary Care Record comes from your GP record. It tells health and care staff:

  • your allergies
  • your medicines
  • any reactions you've had to medicines

It helps health and care staff give you the right care when you are away from your GP surgery. For example, in an emergency.

To use this service you'll need:

  • to be 16 or older
  • an NHS login - if you do not have one, we'll help you set one up
