Use this service to choose whether data from your health records can be used for research and planning.

You can make or change your choice by using our online service or completing a paper form and posting it back to us. You can also ask for help using our telephone service. You must complete a paper form if you wish to make or change a choice on behalf of someone else.

To use this service

You'll need to prove your identity. To do this you can use an NHS login or set one up. You can also provide an email address or phone number registered with an NHS service to use the online service.

Ask your GP surgery for help if you need to confirm your contact information is up-to-date.

You must also be aged 13 or over.
