The different kinds of health records

Health services such as your GP surgery or dentist have their own health record about you.

The different kinds of health records include:

  • GP record
  • Hospital record
  • Vaccinations record
  • Summary Care Record
  • Dental record
  • Optician’s record
  • Sexual health record
  • Mental health record
Information:

You may not have all of these health records. For example, you will only have a sexual health record if you have been to a sexual health clinic.

Your GP record and your vaccinations record

You can look at your GP record and your vaccinations record online using GP online services.

You can ask to set up GP online services at your GP surgery.

Start using GP online services

Your Summary Care Record

Speak to your GP surgery to ask to look at your Summary Care Record.

Some GP online services can show you your Summary Care Record.
