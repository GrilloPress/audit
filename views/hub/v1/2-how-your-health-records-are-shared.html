How your health records are shared for your care

Health and care staff might look at your health records to care for you.

For example, they might look at your Summary Care Record to check if you have any allergies before they give you any medicine.

You can use our service to find who’s looked at your Summary Care Record.

How your health records are shared for secondary purposes

Some of the information from your health records can be used by companies for:

  • research to find cures and better treatments for illnesses
  • planning where we need to improve or provide more health services

You can use our service to choose if your health records are used for research and planning.
