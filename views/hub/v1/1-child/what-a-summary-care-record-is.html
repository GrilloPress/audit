Your Summary Care Record comes from your GP record. It tells health and care staff:

  • your allergies
  • your medicines
  • any reactions you've had to medicines

Health and care staff might look at your Summary Care Record:

  • in an emergency
  • at a walk-in centre
  • at a pharmacy

You can use our online service to find who’s looked at your Summary Care Record.
