Use this service to:

  • check the personal details the NHS has for you
  • see who's looked at your personal details

The NHS has a patient database which includes information such as your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • NHS number

This information is used by the NHS to help identify you and allow services to contact you. For example, to help a hospital text you an appointment reminder.

To use this service you'll need:

  • to be 16 or older
  • an NHS login - if you do not have one, we'll help you set one up
